# Getting Started with Subscriptions

This guide will help you understand and use the subscription features.

## What is a Subscription?

A subscription is a recurring payment arrangement that gives you access to products or services.

## Creating Your First Subscription

1. Navigate to the subscription page
2. Select your desired plan
3. Enter payment information
4. Confirm your subscription

!!! tip
    You can cancel your subscription at any time without penalty.

## Subscription Plans

We offer three types of subscription plans:

- **Basic** - Essential features for individuals
- **Pro** - Advanced features for professionals
- **Enterprise** - Full features for organizations
